Dance Forms

Hip Hop

Energetic and expressive, focusing on street style moves.

Lyrical

Lyrical is choreography inspired directly by the lyrics of a song and is intended to be an interpretation of the song.

Garba

Celebratory dance representing Gujrati cultural traditions.

Jazz

Smooth and fluid movements, combining swing and expression.

Jazzunk

Jazz-funk dance, also called street jazz, is a hybrid of hip-hop and jazz dance.

House

House dance is a freestyle street dance and social dance that has roots in the underground house music scene.

Bollywood

Bollywood dancing is a colourful, dynamic and highly theatrical dance style seen in Indian films.

Semi-classical

Semi-Classical dance blends the grace of classical Indian dance forms with modern, expressive movements, and that is elegant.

Bharatnatyam

Bharatanatyam is a classical Indian dance known for its precise movements, expressive storytelling, and intricate footwork.
